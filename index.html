About Me

I am a Ph.D student in Statistics at School of Data Science, Fudan University, advised by Prof. Weiguo Zheng. I have got a Bachelor's and a Master's degree from Nankai University and Fudan University, respectively, both in Statistics. My research focuses on graph algorithms and graph neural networks.

Research Interests

  • Graph Algorithms
  • Graph Representation
  • Graph Neural Network
